MikeYeung Partners with Gamercraft

April 18, 2021

As Gamercraft continues to rapidly expand, we’re incredibly happy to announce our first influencer partnership with former professional League of Legends Jungler, Mike Yeung! MikeYeung started playing League of Legends using a track pad for three years and at just 13 years old had achieved the Challenger rank! MikeYeung is most known for his time as a player with TSM, Team Liquid, Phoenix 1, and Echofox.


"I'm super excited to be apart of Gamercraft and I'm looking forward to teaching you guys using all the knowledge I've acquired through my years of playing at the highest level" - Mike Yeung


His most notable achievements include winning the Rookie of the Split award in his first season as a professional player, winning the Rift Rivals 2017 Group Stage MVP award and reaching the MSI finals. Since retiring from the LCS, MikeYeung has coached well over 1,000 students through his vibrant community and currently competes in the collegiate League of Legends scene with UCI Esports. 

‍

“We're excited to partner with Mike Yeung because this marks the beginning of a new era for Gamercraft's community. This partnership will build on our mission of building products and communities centered around the needs of competitive amateur gamers, and will also build on Mike's strengths to provide coaching, content, and other avenues of improvement for our users.” - Jose Javier Garcia-Rovira, CEO & Founder

About Gamercraft

Gamercraft is a full-service esports tournament app striving to fill the gap between the amateur players and the professionals by delivering engaging and fair competitive experiences for gamers at all skill levels through our skill-adjusted League of Legends tournaments and leagues. With full game integration, automatic results, AI-powered anti-smurfing detection, and an always evolving set of features, Gamercraft is the go-to app for creating and managing your own team with over $25,000 of prizes (and growing) available monthly through our tournaments.
