Playstyle, combos and tips

As previously mentioned, this update has ensured Mundo has maintained his core identity of a tank that is perpetually in your face and whose kit revolves around using health to cast abilities and regenerate missing health.¬†

The rework has primarily improved Mundo‚Äôs laning phase, and his new E (Blunt Force Trauma) and W (Electrocute) are effective forms at clearing and pushing waves. Mundo‚Äôs Q (Infected Cleaver) hasn‚Äôt really changed much, and still works as a poke tool or to initiate a gank thanks to the slow provided.
