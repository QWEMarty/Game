Role Description

At Gamercraft, we are looking to better the lives of millions of gamers by building the best play-to-earn experiences for competitive gamers. We are a small dynamic team of full-stack developers, committed to creating fantastic competitive and entertainment experiences. We're looking for a full-stack engineer. You will be working closely with the CEO, CTO, and the engineering team. Most of your work will be front-end related. You will have opportunities to work on our backend codebase. This is an awesome opportunity for a young engineer looking to learn more about React Native/TypeScript/React, our main technology stack, and the games we cover. We promise that the job will be fun, dynamic, and definitely not boring or repetitive.

What is our stack?

  • React for Web
  • React Native for Mobile
  • Typescript
  • Serverless on AWS (AWS Lambda, API Gateway, DynamoDB, Step Functions, EventBridge)
  • Node

Requirements: What you should have

  • Familiarity with web technologies and languages (JavaScript/TypeScript)
  • Experience implementing web/mobile user interfaces
  • Bachelor's degree or above in Computer Science/Engineering or prior engineering experience
  • Great design intuition and user empathy
  • Passionate about gaming
  • Entrepreneurial and not afraid to wear different hats
  • Willingness to learn and use new technologies

Bonus Points

  • Familiarity with Serverless technologies and AWS
  • Experience with React

Responsibilities: what you will be doing

  • You would be working with our Founding team on a day-to-day basis
  • Be part of the team building our customer-facing applications
  • Coding front and back end logic, integrating UI and animations
  • Collaborating with our software engineering team, designers, product managers, and user testers
  • Work in an Agile environment to understand requirements, estimate the level of effort, and plan out the development roadmap

The Gamercraft Story

Gamercraft is creating the new standard for online gaming experiences, starting with tournaments. We internalize the creation, organization, and production of online gaming tournaments.
