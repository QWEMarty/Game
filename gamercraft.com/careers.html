Work at Gamercraft

Mission

Gamercraft is creating the new standard for online gaming experiences, starting with tournaments. We are using technology to create the most simple, fun, and immersive experiences while solving many of the problems gamers and families face today.

Vision

We are here to help gamers achieve their goals, live their passions, improve their skills, grow their potential, and have access to the latest technologies. We want to allow players to live their gaming dreams in a healthy, competitive, and non-toxic way.

Benefits of
working with us

We go the extra mile in everything we do, including benefits. We offer one of the industry's most competitive and fun compensation and benefits packages. From salaries to work-life-balance to supporting your hobbies and gaming passions, our offerings were designed to keep employees happy and motivated.

Upgraded Gaming Benefits

We are gamers, and work-life balance is essential to us. Every month, we give our employees a monthly budget for gaming and gaming-related purchases such as gift cards, keyboards, mouses, and more.
‍

Competitive Compensation

We offer competitive, location-adjusted compensation packages that are equal or above to the gaming industry standard. We also have processes to ensure that the comp package remains competitive over time.

Health Insurance & Wellness

We offer complete and comprehensive dental, vision, and health insurance plans to all of our employees. We want our team to be healthy, focused, and stress-free.


Value-oriented Culture

We put passion and intelligence behind all of our decisions. Our company culture is one of innovation, resiliency, focus, and fast execution. We’re driven by competition, curiosity, and by actively testing and conversing with users.
